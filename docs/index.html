About Us

Sunshine SMP is a Minecraft server that was started in January 2022 by four friends named Mit, Zaid, Ankit, and Lance. The server is a place where these friends can come together to play Minecraft and build their virtual world. The group has a love for playing Minecraft, and they wanted to create a space where they could explore, build, and have fun together.

Sunshine SMP view from SizeableOdin287's rooftop

Sunshine SMP has become a place where the four friends can connect with each other, share their love for the game, and create something truly unique.

One of the most exciting things about Sunshine SMP is the fact that each member has built their own house. This allows each player to have their own space to call home and decorate as they please. Each house has its own unique style and design, which showcases the creativity and individuality of each player. This not only adds to the beauty of the server, but it also fosters a sense of pride and ownership for each member.

Looking ahead, Sunshine SMP has big plans for the future. The group is excited to continue building their virtual world, and they have plans to add more houses and structures in the coming months. Additionally, they are looking to invite new players to join their server and become part of their community. Sunshine SMP is a place where friends can come together, build their dreams, and have fun in the process.
